My Favorite Places

Hong Kong is definitley one of my favorite places that I have ever traveled to in my whole life. The place itslef is absolutley beutiful, the beaches were outsanding along with the sunny weather. The food thewre is what drove me crazy the mosg. I felt like I was in Hollywood as I walked up the streets. I remeber going to many old castles which were absolutley unforegettable. Even though it is very qujite and calm, I highly recommend visiting this city.
